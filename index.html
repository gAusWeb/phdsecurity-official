Corporate Security Services

Our team will become part of the security blanket you install to protect your people and equipment. We provide a team capable of utilising the business security systems and services made available while providing vigilant oversight.

Building & Apartment Services

Asset Protection Services

With over 35 years’ experience we have a strong network of professionals we can make available to advise you on the best cameras & monitoring systems and services to deliver a bespoke level of support for your business. Security systems can be expensive, our advice and oversight can reduce costs and ensure wise investment is made.

Our staff are trained to work with all systems however when only the best will do, we will tailor it to suit your budget.

Personal Security Services

We understand the different needs of a security guard and enjoy strong relationships with agencies specialising in high-level individual security. We outsource the protection of high, at-risk clients requiring gun licences. Servicing all other levels is our specialty. If you have VIP’s requiring personal oversight our staff will ensure their comfort and safety.
